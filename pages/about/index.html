Our Story

Handloom Soul. Digital Precision.

The Print Loom was founded on a simple idea: the artistry of India's handloom traditions deserves a canvas that keeps up with modern life — colorfast, detailed, and made for real days, not just showcases.

The Spark

Started as a small studio experimenting with digital printing on traditional saree fabrics.

The Craft

Refined our print process across silk, georgette, linen, organza and cotton for true color depth.

The Standard

Introduced hand quality-checks on every saree before it leaves our studio.

Today

Shipping across India with guest checkout, COD, and secure online payments.

Our Promise

Every Print Tells a Story

We design every motif in-house, drawing from classic Indian textile patterns — bandhani, ikat, temple borders, and paisley — reinterpreted for a digital canvas. Each saree is inspected by hand before it's packed, because a print is only premium if it survives the wear.
